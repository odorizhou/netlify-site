Intro

This page shows game stats of custom KataGo Bots based on v1.16.4 with 18 block NN trained on handicap games.

The bots play on KGS server, accepting 2 stone to 7 stone handicap games against human players ranked from 5k to 7d with time setting of 15 min + 5x30s.
